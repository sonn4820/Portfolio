Pandemonium

Itch.IO Trello GitHub Trailer

Pandemonium is a Cyberpunk themed, Third-Person Shooter with a focus on whacky physics and incredible destruction. Feel like your own action hero as you tackle challenging enemies with real sound-sampled weapons. Blow them away with an expressive and exaggerated ragdoll system that launches enemies around the map. Experiment with your tactics and explore different routes in a map inspired by classic action cinema. Watch the world crumble around you as objects procedurally break apart and reflect the chaos around you. Above all we are committed to producing and fun and replayable core loop that we hope will provide incentive for players to keep coming back for more.

    My Contributions:
  • Researching and programming the ragdoll and physics mechanics
  • Adding animation and physics mechanics to 3D model
  • Programming the enemy's mechanics and physics related mechanics
  • Balancing stats and gameplay
