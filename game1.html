Orbit

Itch.IO Trello GitHub

"Orbits" is one of my favorite games. Inspired by Doodle Jump and vertical scroller games, as well as the meme "The floor is lava," my teammate and I decided to combine gravity and the spinning wheel into one, and thus Orbits was born. A casual game aimed at the mobile market. This game was created in less than 56 hours using Unity. Orbit isn't just about jumping; the player may need prediction skills to calculate the landing point as well as the spinning orbit's speed. I appreciate your skill and effort if you can beat all three levels.

My Contributions:

  • Lead Programmer and Lead Developer
  • Programming the orbit mechanic and UI/UX interaction
  • Creating art assets for the player and UI/UX
  • Researching and collecting background art and audio
