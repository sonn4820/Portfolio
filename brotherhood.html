Brotherhood

Itch.IO RPI Gamefest 2020

Brotherhood marked a significant milestone, being the first game for which I was honored with an award - the prestigious RPI Gamfest 2020 Innovation Award. It was also the inaugural project where I utilized the PuzzleScript engine as my development platform. This puzzle game presents a unique challenge to players, requiring them to simultaneously control multiple characters. Each character possesses the ability to vanquish a specific type of enemy, compelling players to strategically deploy them to solve intricate puzzles. An added layer of depth comes from the hidden powers of each character, waiting to be uncovered by the inquisitive player. The game underwent an extensive iterative process, undergoing numerous revisions based on valuable feedback from playtesters, ultimately resulting in an overwhelmingly positive reception.

Game Detail:

  • Engine & Tool: PuzzleScript
  • Development Time: 4 months
