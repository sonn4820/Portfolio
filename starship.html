Starship

Download GitHub

Lost in the cold void of space, you and your friend drifted aimlessly until their ship ran fatally dry of fuel. With a gut-wrenching choice, you had to leave them behind to brave unspeakable dangers in search of the nearest refueling station - their only hope of salvation. You battled terror after terror in that infinite obsidian expanse. But just as salvation seemed within reach, a cataclysmic cosmic event shattered your path, leaving you adrift once more in that uncaring abyss as your friend's life essence ebbed away. The universe seemed hellbent on tormenting you, perhaps demanding a sacrifice greater than any soul should endure.

Game Detail:

  • Engine & Tool: My C++ Engine, OpenGL
  • Development Time: 2 months
