About me

From a young age, I harbored an intense fascination for video games. Growing up in Hanoi, Vietnam, however, opportunities to indulge in this passion were scarce, as my parents disapproved of gaming. Upon arriving in the United States, I found myself immersed in the world of video games, spending my free time exploring various titles. Unlike most, I didn't merely enjoy the games; I found myself captivated by the underlying mechanics that evoked such profound emotions, fueling an insatiable desire to delve deeper. This introspective curiosity ignited my journey towards becoming a game developer, driven by an unquenchable thirst to unravel the secrets behind the immersive experiences that video games offer.
